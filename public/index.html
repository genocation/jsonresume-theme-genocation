RideIT (a product by Collcon Labs Pvt. Ltd.) is an Indian startup operating on the urban transport sector. RideIT as a holistic transport solution offers a wide range of features to falicitate public or private vehicle sharing to our customers - as part of its B2C vertical -, and offers full transport and logistic solutions to companies - B2B. RideIT approaches the challenge of indian transport sharing by specializing on a smart route-based matching algorithm, maximizing the relevance of search results, collecting information of the daily and hourly rides happening in urban areas and generating methods of data analysis to understand the transport flows.
